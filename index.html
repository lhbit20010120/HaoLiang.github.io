Guo Lu

Assistant Professor

Beijing Institute of Technology


I am an assistant professor at the Beijing Institute of Technology. Before this, I obtained my Ph.D degree in the Department of Electronic Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong Univrsity at 2020. From Sep. 2017 to Mar. 2019, I was a visiting student in the University of Sydney and work with Dong Xu and Wanli Ouyang . I received the B. Eng. degree in the Department of Electronic Engineering, Ocean University of China, 2014. My research interests focus on video compression, video processing and machine learning. In particular, I proposed the first end-to-end optimized video codec. Here is my CV(中文简历).
